💕 Character Routes & Romance Guide

Master all four romance paths with our detailed character analysis and choice strategies

🌸 Sakura Route

Shy Artistic Gentle Creative

A talented artist struggling with self-confidence. Sakura responds well to patience, encouragement, and showing genuine interest in her art.

Key Strategy: Choose gentle, supportive responses. Ask about her artwork and dreams. Avoid pushy or aggressive choices.

Best Ending Requirement: Attend all her art exhibitions and consistently choose creative dialogue options.

⚡ Alex Route

Athletic Confident Loyal Direct

Star athlete with a competitive spirit but hidden vulnerabilities. Values honesty, loyalty, and direct communication above all.

Key Strategy: Be straightforward and honest. Support their goals and show up when you promise to. Avoid being indirect or wishy-washy.

Best Ending Requirement: Win the trust challenge in Chapter 5 and consistently choose loyalty-focused options.

📚 Morgan Route

Intellectual Bookworm Thoughtful Witty

Brilliant student who loves deep conversations and intellectual challenges. Appreciates wit, literary knowledge, and thoughtful analysis.

Key Strategy: Engage in meaningful discussions. Reference books, philosophy, and current events. Show your intellectual curiosity.

Best Ending Requirement: Successfully complete the library research project together and choose all philosophical dialogue options.

🎭 Riley Route

Mysterious Performer Complex Guarded

Enigmatic theater performer with a complicated past. Requires patience to break through their emotional barriers and earn their trust.

Key Strategy: Show genuine interest in their performances. Be non-judgmental about their past. Demonstrate that you accept them completely.

Best Ending Requirement: Attend every performance and unlock the "True Past" revelation in Chapter 7.

💡 Pro Romance Tips

Save Before Major Choices: Create multiple save files before important decisions. Each character has 2-3 critical choice points that determine your ending.

Character Consistency: Stay true to your chosen character's personality type. Mixed signals often lead to friendship endings rather than romance.

Hidden Affection Points: Some choices give hidden affection boosts. Generally, choices that show you remember personal details about your chosen character give bonus points.

📋 Complete Walkthrough Guide

Step-by-step guide through every chapter with all critical choice points

Chapter 1: First Impressions

Setting: School festival - Your first day at the new school

Critical Choice: "Where do you want to go first at the festival?"

  • "The art exhibition looks interesting" → Opens Sakura route + 2 affection
  • "I want to check out the sports demonstrations" → Opens Alex route + 2 affection
  • "Is there a quiet place to read or study?" → Opens Morgan route + 2 affection
  • "The theater performance starts soon" → Opens Riley route + 2 affection

Important: This choice determines which character notices you first and affects all future interactions.

Chapter 2: Getting to Know You

Setting: First one-on-one conversation with your chosen character

Universal Strategy: Ask follow-up questions about their interests. Avoid generic responses like "That's nice" or "Cool."

Sakura Path: Ask about her art techniques and inspiration sources

Alex Path: Show interest in their training regimen and athletic goals

Morgan Path: Discuss books, theories, or current events

Riley Path: Ask about their acting experience and favorite roles

Chapter 3: Trust Building

Major Choice Point: Each character will share something personal and ask for advice

Golden Rule: Always choose the response that validates their feelings while offering constructive support

  • Sakura's Art Crisis: "Your art speaks to people in ways you don't even realize" (Best response)
  • Alex's Competition Anxiety: "You've trained hard for this. Trust in your preparation" (Best response)
  • Morgan's Academic Pressure: "Intelligence isn't just about grades. You think deeply about things" (Best response)
  • Riley's Past Revelation: "Thank you for trusting me with this. It doesn't change how I see you" (Best response)

Chapter 5: The Relationship Test

Critical Decision Point: Each character will present you with a challenge or test of commitment

Warning: This chapter determines if you can access the best endings. Wrong choices here lock you out of perfect endings.

Success Requirements:

  • Sakura: Attend her art exhibition despite other commitments
  • Alex: Show up to their important game, even if you have conflicts
  • Morgan: Complete the collaborative research project thoroughly
  • Riley: Attend their solo performance and stay for the after-party

Chapter 7: Climax & Resolution

Final Choice: Each route has a crucial final decision that determines your ending

Best Ending Requirements: Must have made correct choices in Chapters 1, 3, and 5, plus maintain high affection throughout

🎯 Walkthrough Pro Tips

Affection System: Track your relationship progress through dialogue cues. Characters will be more open and friendly as affection increases.

Multiple Playthroughs: Some achievements require seeing multiple endings. Start with your preferred character, then branch out.

Secret Scenes: High affection unlocks bonus CG scenes and extended dialogue options in later chapters.

🏆 Complete Endings Guide

Unlock all 12+ endings with our detailed requirements and strategies

💖 Best Endings (True Love Routes)

Requirements: Perfect choice sequence + maximum affection + completion of character-specific challenges

  • Sakura True End: Support her art career decision + attend all exhibitions + choose creative dialogue options throughout
  • Alex True End: Win trust challenge + support athletic dreams + maintain honesty in all interactions
  • Morgan True End: Complete research project + engage in all intellectual discussions + choose thoughtful responses
  • Riley True End: Attend all performances + unlock past revelation + choose accepting dialogue options

💙 Good Endings (Happy Together)

Requirements: Good relationship but missed some optimization choices

These endings show a positive relationship future but without the deep emotional resolution of True Endings. Generally achieved by maintaining medium-high affection but missing 1-2 critical choices.

💛 Friendship Endings

Requirements: Low romantic affection but maintained positive relationship

Occurs when you show interest in multiple characters or consistently choose neutral responses. Characters remain close friends but no romance develops.

💔 Bad Endings

Triggers: Consistently poor choices or neglecting your chosen character

Generally result from: ignoring character events, choosing responses that contradict their values, or failing major trust challenges.

🌟 Secret Endings

Hidden Requirements: Special unlock conditions

  • True Solo Ending: Deliberately avoid all romantic options and focus on self-growth
  • Everyone's Friend: Maintain equal affection with all characters (very difficult balance)
  • New Game+ Ending: Available only after completing at least 3 different character routes
  • Creator's Ending: Find and interact with hidden easter eggs throughout multiple playthroughs

🎭 Ending Collection Strategy

Recommended Order: Start with your favorite character's True Ending, then work backwards through their other endings before moving to a new character.

Save Management: Keep saves before major choice points in each chapter. This allows you to quickly branch to different endings without full replays.

Achievement Tracking: Some endings unlock bonus content or special scenes in subsequent playthroughs.
