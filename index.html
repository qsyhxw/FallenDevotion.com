Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fallen Devotion free to download?

Yes, Fallen Devotion is currently available as a free demo on itch.io for Windows, macOS, and Linux. The full version is in development. See our download guide for installation instructions.

What platforms is the game available on?

Fallen Devotion supports Windows, macOS, and Linux. The game is optimized for desktop/laptop play. Mobile versions are not currently available. Check the system requirements to ensure compatibility.

How many endings are in the demo?

The current demo features 2-3 playable endings, with more planned for the full release. Each ending depends on the choices you make throughout the story. Our complete endings guide explains how to unlock each route.

Who is Clive Donovan?

Clive Donovan is the main character - your deceased childhood best friend who has mysteriously returned after three years. His character embodies themes of eternal return and the Dionysian spirit from Nietzsche's philosophy. Read our complete character analysis.

What is the connection to Nietzsche's philosophy?

The game is inspired by Nietzsche's works, particularly "Thus Spoke Zarathustra" and "The Birth of Tragedy." Characters and story arcs explore concepts like the Apollonian vs Dionysian, eternal return, guilt, and redemption. Our philosophy guide breaks down these connections in detail.

When will the full game be released?

The full release date has not been officially announced yet. The developer (billetdoux) is publishing updates gradually, including voice acting additions. Follow our development updates page for the latest news.

Are there similar games I might like?

Yes! If you enjoy Fallen Devotion, check out other yandere and dark romance visual novels like "Your Boyfriend," "Doki Doki Literature Club," and "Boyfriend to Death." See our complete recommendations list with comparisons.

Is the game suitable for all ages?

No. Fallen Devotion is recommended for ages 18+ due to mature themes including psychological thriller elements, dark romance, and complex philosophical content. The game includes content warnings for themes that may be distressing.

How do I unlock all endings?

Each ending requires specific dialogue choices throughout the game. Key decisions affect your relationship with Clive and determine which ending you reach. Our endings guide provides step-by-step walkthroughs and an interactive choice flowchart.

Where can I find the official developer?

The developer billetdoux is active on itch.io and Tumblr. For updates, behind-the-scenes content, and developer insights, follow the official Tumblr account.
