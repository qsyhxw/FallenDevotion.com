Fallen Devotion Characters: Clive, Dariel & Known Cast Notes

This page organizes what is publicly knowable from the current demo and official pages. Clive has the strongest information base, Dariel has a limited but playable presence, and several named characters remain low-information entries.

Information Notice: This index separates confirmed demo details, limited official details, and information that should not be treated as confirmed yet.

Quick Answer

The current Fallen Devotion demo is centered most strongly on Clive Donovan. Dariel has enough demo presence and player discussion to justify a separate page, but the information is intentionally limited. Julian, Silas, and Mia are best handled as low-information cast entries until official sources give clearer public details.

How to Read Character Claims

  • Confirmed: visible in the current demo, official itch.io page, official posts, or clearly repeated official source material.
  • Limited: supported by demo/player context but missing full official explanation.
  • Theory: useful for fan discussion, but separated from facts on pages like Fan Theories.

Why the Index Stays Conservative

A character page can rank well without pretending every cast member has the same amount of public information. This index is strongest when it clearly shows where Clive and Dariel have enough substance, and where Julian, Silas, and Mia should remain short cast notes.
