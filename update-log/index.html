About the Developer

billetdoux (Developer)

Fallen Devotion is billetdoux's first visual novel project. Despite having no prior coding experience, the developer has created a compelling narrative experience that explores complex philosophical themes inspired by Nietzsche's work.

Role: Writer, Artist, Developer

First VN: Yes (learning as they go)

Philosophy Background: Self-described "philosophy nerd"

Development Team

  • Voice Acting Director: helloyinny (casting and voice direction)
  • Music Composer: onemansymphony (original soundtrack)
  • Development Approach: Solo indie project with collaborators

Developer's Philosophy:

"I wanted to explore themes like eternal return, choices, and the fine line between love and obsession."

Frequently Asked Questions

Will the full game be free like the demo?
This has not been officially confirmed. While the demo is free, the developer may choose to price the full version to support continued development. Any pricing decisions will be announced on official channels.
Will my demo save file work with the full version?
Save file compatibility has not been confirmed. It's possible that due to engine updates or structural changes, you may need to restart when the full version releases. Keep an eye on official announcements for clarification.
How can I support the developer?
Share the game with friends, leave positive reviews on itch.io, create fan art (tag @fallendev0tionvn on Tumblr), and provide constructive feedback. The developer welcomes community engagement and appreciation for the work.
Why is development taking so long?
This is a solo indie project by a first-time developer learning game development from scratch. Creating a visual novel involves writing, art, programming, voice direction, and more. The developer prioritizes quality and sustainable development pace over rushing to release.
Will there be more characters beyond Clive?
Yes! The full version will expand on other characters like Dariel, Julian, Silas, and Mia, who are introduced in the demo. Expect deeper character routes and additional romantic/narrative paths.
What platforms will the full game support?
Based on the demo, expect Windows, macOS, and Linux support. Mobile versions have not been announced. Check official channels for any platform expansion updates.
Can I help with development (testing, translation, etc.)?
The developer has not publicly requested volunteer assistance, but you can reach out via Tumblr messages or itch.io comments to offer specific skills. Be respectful of their time and workflow.
